# Curriculum

## **Phase 0: Setup & Tooling**

**Goal:** Prepare your environment to run algorithms locally

**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  **Python** (loops, functions, variables)
- [ ]  **Git** (for version control)
- [ ]  **Terminal** navigation on your Mac

## **Phase I: Math Foundations**

**Goal:** Learn how to derive the math required to "teach" an AI

**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  **Linear Algebra** (Vectors, Matrices, Dot Products, Broadcasting)
- [ ]  **Calculus** (Derivatives, Partial Derivatives, Chain Rule)

## **Phase II: ML Fundamentals**

**Goal:** Build your first predictive models from scratch

**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  **NumPy** for numerical computing
- [ ]  **Linear Regression**
- [ ]  **Classification**
- [ ]  Gradient Descent

## **Phase III: Deep Learning Core**

**Goal:** Understand how information flows through a "brain"
**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  **Neural Network** architecture (Perceptrons, Hidden Layers).
- [ ]  **Activations** (ReLU, Sigmoid, Tanh)
- [ ]  **Backpropagation**

## **Phase IV & V: Computer Vision & NLP Foundations**

**Goal:** Learn how AI processes images and text before the era of Transformers
**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  **PyTorch** (framework entry)
- [ ]  NLP concepts
- [ ]  **Tokenization**
- [ ]  **Word Embeddings**

## **Phase VI: Speech & Audio**

**Goal:** Essential for adding voice-command features to your Mac agent
**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  Audio processing
- [ ]  signal analysis

## **Phase VII & VIII: Transformers & Generative AI**

**Goal:** Build the blueprint used by models like Claude and GPT
**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  The **Transformer Architecture**
- [ ]  **Attention Mechanism** (Query, Key, Value)
- [ ]  **Multi-Head Attention**
- [ ]  **Positional Encoding**

## **Phase IX: Reinforcement Learning (RL)**

**Goal:** Learn how models like DeepSeek-R1 "reason" through self-correction

**Tech to Learn:** 

- [ ]  Trial-and-error reward systems

## **Phase X & XI: LLM Engineering**

**Goal:** Optimize a large model to run efficiently on your MacBook M1
**Tech to Learn:** 

- [ ]  **Fine-tuning** (Full and **LoRA**)
- [ ]  **Quantization** (4-bit/8-bit to fit models on your 16GB RAM)
- [ ]  **Inference** optimization (KV Cache, Temperature)

## **Phase XII – XIV: Multimodal & Agent Engineering**

**Goal:** Turn a "chatbot" into an "agent" that performs actions on your system
**Tech to Learn:** 

- [ ]  **Tool Use**
- [ ]  **Planning**
- [ ]  **Memory**
- [ ]  Use **LangChain**
- [ ]  **CrewAI** to connect your AI to Mac-specific "Tools" (querying files, opening apps)

## **Phase XV & XVI: Autonomous Systems & Swarms**

**Goal:** Enable multiple AI agents to work together on complex business tasks
**Tech to Learn:** 

- [ ]  Multi-agent coordination and complex autonomous loops

## **Phase XVII & XVIII: Infrastructure, Safety & Ethics**

**Goal:** Ensure your "Security-First" agent follows your ethical rules and operates locally with high speed
**Tech to Learn:**

- [ ]  **Distributed Training**
- [ ]  **RAG** (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
- [ ]  **Constitutional AI** (programming a "constitution" for safety)

## **Phase XIX: Capstone Projects**

**Goal:** Finalize the build of your proprietary Mac app (e.g., **Endronic** or **Aletheic**) that controls your OS privately
**Tech to Learn:** 

- [ ]  Integration of all previous phases
